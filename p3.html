Project 3

The overall approach I took for this project is to connect with one of my favorite shows called dragon ball z and give food to my cat. Which took the idea of adding one core important to the shows, which is seven dragon balls, each having a number set of stars in the ball up to seven stars. I take this symbol of dragons and make it into sets of seconds, min and hours. Each section has a different color type from other sections, this different color is not just for show but also represents moments/events during dragon ball z. For example,the cause of change to be blue/black dragon is due to many people using the dragon balls for mainly bad reasons/negative use of dragons that used to be us, which used it for good. For the left side of the screen, showcase a bowl that fills up with cat food, which goes by mill seconds, also at a certain points during hours, going pop up saying cat, eat the food now, which happens one hour during the entire 24 hours. The overall design from the dark background and white floor is me trying to remake the location that dragon balls are mostly used in the show(which is a big tower, high up in the hair). Also the green long rect in the background is me trying to show the long length of the dragon that comes from dragon balls after using it. Made parts I struggle the most is heavily trying to make counting time through limit of 7 starts, cause me to do lots of different idea of how i can 10 number in 7 number, which i end up doing overlay/stacking on top so end up representing 10 numbers
