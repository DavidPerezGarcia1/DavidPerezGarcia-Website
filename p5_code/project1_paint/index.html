Project 1

Glitch brush makes random glitch rectangle from up,down and left to right, when angle, shape break apart to X like shape,
right click and move your mouse to other side of the canvas and click, it will make on single stroke of color.
Depending on how fast you move your mouse,
less glitch action will see, from fast to be simple, from slow being more glitch.

Angles smooth brush makes peach like color, which angle on top left on screen, turn red and expand to skin like color, which repeats.

Spinning blue brush makes a random length of a brush that spins around at top left, when holding brush on top of the canvas, it is more circle shaped.
However, on left side of canvas, become more outward brush unlike circle like shape

Press 1 to use glitch brush

Press 2 to use agnle smooth brush

Press 3 to use spinning blue brush

Press q to use eraser

Press e to remove all from screen
