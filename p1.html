Also can be complex in a way if the user wants a certain movement and shape from the brush. From all angles and movement from each of 3 brushes, it is much different but at the same time it’s almost similar in a way. The way I achieve in my opinion is to break everything. If it normally works, I break the code again and again, over and over again.
